GO! STEM

What is GO! STEM?

GO! STEM Summer Program held at Toronto's Salesforce office and it focuses on empowering girls ages 13-17 to explore and study career paths in STEM. This year 30 amazing girls participated in the program learning and creating their own website.

What Girls Say About GO! STEM:

Aisha Mohamud | 16

"I never thought that I would pursue a career in STEM, but now thanks to GO! STEM it has influenced me to think about going into Computer Programming. I am thinking of ways to combine American Sign Language (ASL) and tech. Thank you GO! STEM and hEr VOLUTION for the amazing experience!"

Daniela Uribe | 13

"I didn't know what coding was in the beginning. After I've been in this program for the past week, I found that coding could be extremely fun and interesting while being to create my own webpage. I recommend everyone to do this program!"

Madhu Manivannan | 14

"During GO! STEM, I got to meet a lot of girls with similar interests to mine, which in turn lets me now know a strong community that enpowers me and other girls to take initiative and action to empower others in return. I think the camp has helped me a lot in finding a mentor and meeting a lot of awesome people in 4 days. Thanks hEr VOLUTION for the great experience!"
